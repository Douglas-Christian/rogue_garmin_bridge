Connection Help

If you're having trouble connecting to your Rogue Echo equipment:

  1. Put your device in connection mode:
    • For Echo Bike: Press and hold the "Connect" button for 2 seconds until you hear two beeps. The Bluetooth and ANT+ icons should flash on the console to indicate it's ready to pair.
    • For Echo Rower: From the home screen, select the "Connect" option, then choose "Connect to App". The console will display that it's ready to pair.
  2. Make sure your device is powered on and in range
  3. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your computer
  4. Try restarting your Rogue Echo equipment
  5. Check if the device is already connected to another application

For Rogue Echo Bike, start pedaling to wake up the device before connecting.

For Rogue Echo Rower, start rowing to wake up the device before connecting.

When successfully connected, the Bluetooth icon on your device's console should stop flashing and remain solid.
